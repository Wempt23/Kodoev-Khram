About Forrest Gump

Movie Review

Forrest Gump is a timeless classic that seamlessly blends drama, humor, and heart. Tom Hanks delivers an iconic performance as the titular character—a man with a low IQ but an extraordinary ability to influence the course of history. The film brilliantly weaves together decades of American history, from the civil rights movement to the Vietnam War, all through the innocent yet profound perspective of Forrest.

What makes this film special is its message: life is like a box of chocolates, and the journey matters more than the destination. The supporting cast, particularly Sally Field and Gary Sinise, bring depth and emotion to their roles. Director Robert Zemeckis crafts a narrative that is both entertaining and deeply moving, reminding us that kindness, persistence, and love can overcome any obstacle.

Rating: ★★★★★ (5/5) — A must-watch masterpiece.
